# Tips n Tricks


To install and freeze the version of your gems you might use a Gemfile.
Example:

~~~
source 'https://rubygems.org'

gem 'activerecord', '~> 4.2.0'
gem 'activesupport', '~> 4.2.0'    ### check not really needed ?? pulled in by activerecord ??

gem 'worlddb', '~> 2.2.1'
gem 'sportdb', '~> 1.11.0'
~~~
